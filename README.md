# Note Taking App


![MIT License Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-blue)


## Description

Notetaking app that let's you create, save, delete notes as needed.

## Installation

Open the project folder in your terminal and run 'npm install'.


## Usage

If you would like to run the app locally, open the project folder in your terminal then run 'node sever.js'. You can then navigate to http://localhost:3001 in your browser to see the app. You can change the port number inside the server.js if you wish, then find it at whatever port you set. 

Otherwise you can use the app in your browser at https://week-11-express-project.onrender.com/

To use the app, click on the "Get Started" button on the landing page.

<img width="760" height="460" alt="Screenshot 2026-01-22 at 10 04 45 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/aff5fe82-51c4-4552-b997-a38b8bf0bfbc" />


Once inside the Notes page, click on the Test Note at the top to begin creating a new one. 

<img width="449" height="232" alt="Screenshot 2026-01-22 at 10 10 16 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3cca60a8-5ec3-4466-847c-afe22ea3a046" />


After filling out the Title and Body you can click on 'Save Note' in the top right to add the note to the notes bar on the left side, or you can click clear form to erase everything you have entered.

<img width="1496" height="345" alt="Screenshot 2026-01-22 at 10 08 12 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/71f42701-24c0-4379-9c77-be3176f8a5ea" />


You can then access all your created notes on the left side bar. Use the trash can icon on each note to delete the relevant one.

<img width="508" height="465" alt="Screenshot 2026-01-22 at 10 22 20 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/93e6d1bb-2345-4470-90eb-db517393af59" />
